My photo with Nigel Farage

« back

In this photo you can see a happy Nigel Farage standing right next to myself and Ariel Chis, two Romanian students in the UK. This photo was taken at the Arrivals gate of the Entzheim Strasbourg Airport while he was on his way to greet the Pope at the EU Parliament.

This is not a photobomb nor is it faked or edited in any way. We went, ourselves, and asked him if he’d do us the pleasure of taking a photo with us. As simple as that. Now, “Why is he so happy?” you might ask. The answer is he couldn’t tell we are Romanians. We told him that, shortly after taking the picture, at which point he quickly ran away.
